Introduction

On videos you can set cue points that cause Beacon to display a button for the viewer to skip a video's introduction and/or credits. You have the option to set four points in a video that control when the appropriate button appears:

  • skip_intro_start
  • skip_intro_end
  • skip_credits_start
  • skip_credits_end

The following list the behavior of the points and buttons:

  • skip intro end, and not start, beginning of video assumed to be begin
  • skip credit start no end, end assumed to be end of video
  • If end comes before start, nothing happens
  • Duplication of points the second one ignored
  • No validation at this time
  • Pre-roll and post-roll ads will play
  • by default the button will appear for 10 seconds, can change contact Brightcove's OTT Delivery Team

Adding cue points

  1. Log in to Beacon Studio.
  2. Go to the Media module.
  3. Click the video on which you wish to implement skip functionality. This will display the video's details.
